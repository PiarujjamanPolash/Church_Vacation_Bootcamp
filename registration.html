VACATION Boot Camp REGISTRATION POLICY

ELIGIBILITY FOR PARTICIPATION

Requirements for the Elementary Program:


Children who have completed 3rd through 5th grade are eligible to participate in the VBC elementary program.

Language:

All children who attend must be able to communicate effectively in English. It is imperative that they are able to understand directions and express their needs. Foreign language interpretation is not available.

REGISTRATION PROCESS

Registration Form:

A completed registration form is required for each child attending VBC. The registration form must be completely filled out by the child’s parent or guardian prior to a child’s participation in VBC. Registration can be done via hardcopy or online at www.newgateintl.org. Registration forms will be available in the church office.

Payment:

The cost of VBC, as indicated on the registration form, is $30 registration and $100 for the week. Registration is non-refundable and both registration and Camp costs must be paid prior to attending the class. This helps to cover some of the expenses that New Gate International Church incurs to host VBC each summer. In order to be fully registered, the registrar must receive payment either by check (made out to New Gate International Church), credit/debit card, or cash. Online credit/debit card payment can be made at www.newgateintl.org.

Payment for a Partial Week:

Children are highly encouraged to attend the full week of VBC. Each day is important as the children build relationships with others in their crews. The cost per child for New Gate International Church is not divided up into daily amounts; it is an overall expense thus no discount is offered for children who only attend for a partial week.

Scholarships:

A child’s spiritual formation is extremely important, however at this time New Gate International Church is not able to offer scholarships. We do plan on a having a scholarship program possibly in 2021.
